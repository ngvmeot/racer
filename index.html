Privacy Policy for NGV Racer

Last updated: June 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information used within our mobile application. This application operates entirely offline regarding user data.

1. Information Used and Processed (Local Only)

Our application does not require any account registration or server-side storage. To provide bicycle computer functionalities and calculate metrics, the application processes the following data:

  • Fitness Metrics: You may optionally enter your weight and heart rate values directly into the application. This data is used solely to calculate power-to-weight ratios (W/kg) and to define heart rate training zones.
  • Location Data (GPS): The application requires access to your device's GPS/location services to compute real-time ride metrics (such as speed, distance, and elapsed time) and to record your cycling tracks. This allows the app to function properly when external speed sensors are unavailable or disconnected, and to export your completed rides as standard workout files (such as .fit files).
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: The application requires Bluetooth permissions (specifically to scan for and connect to nearby devices) strictly to discover and receive real-time training data from your external cycling sensors (such as heart rate monitors, power meters, speed, or cadence sensors). We do not use Bluetooth for location tracking, beacons, or any marketing purposes, and this data never leaves your device.

Crucial Note: All fitness metrics, location data, and calculated ride history are processed and stored strictly within the application's isolated local sandbox or user-designated folders on your mobile device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cloud services that track or collect your information.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data and recorded workout files are stored exclusively on your device, you have complete control over them. You can delete your recorded rides or modify your fitness metrics within the application settings at any time. Uninstalling the application from your device will automatically and permanently delete all local app-specific data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
